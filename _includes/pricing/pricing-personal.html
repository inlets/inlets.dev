Supporting developers

What's the difference between business and personal?

The personal license can be used at work or at home with your home-lab, we give you two tunnels so that you can run one at home and still use inlets at PRO. The business license unlocks commercial support and other restrictions on use.

You can use your license for any kind of development. However, only you may use your personal license; you may not share it with others. Your company cannot pay, reimburse, or in any way finance your personal license.

What is acceptable use for a personal license? Anything that you as an individual may do in your daily workflow to achieve your job as an individual. In other words, this license should not be used by other people in your company, or as part of a company system or permanent integration. You also need to buy this with your own money and personal email address.

  • Receiving webhooks during development
  • Hosting personal projects and websites.
  • Remote access to services like RDP, VNC, SSH.
  • Sharing work or a blog post draft with a client or colleague.
  • To get a static IP when you're behind NAT, have a dynamic IP, or when ports are blocked.
  • Exposing a self-hosted service from a Kubernetes cluster for development and testing.
  • Exposing dashboards or services from your Raspberry Pi.
  • To avoid rate-limits and corporate banning of SaaS tunnels like Ngrok.
