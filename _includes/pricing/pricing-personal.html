Do I need Personal or Professional?

Personal can only be purchased with a personal email and personal card. It cannot be expensed or shared.

Professional is required for usage by or within a business. It can be expensed and shared with team-mates.

Can I use Personal to evaluate inlets for my business?
You will require Professional, however you can buy 1-2 licenses initially for experimentation, on a monthly basis.
Can I use Personal to set up a permanent tunnel or integration?
You may only do this at home, for personal usage, not at work or for your employer.
How do I expense Personal?
You must pay for Personal with your own money, and it cannot be expensed. Professional can be expensed.
What email address can I use for Personal?
You'll need to use a personal email address for Personal. We reserve the right to cancel any personal licenses bought with a company email address.
How many people can I share Personal with?
Only Professional can be shared between multiple people, as long as no more tunnels are launched than specified in your license.
